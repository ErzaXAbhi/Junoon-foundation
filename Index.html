About Junoon Foundation

Junoon Foundation is a Thane-based non-profitable organization working in the education sector. We have more than 10 live welfare projects on education, healthcare, livelihood, and women’s empowerment in over 20 remote villages across Palghar & Thane district.

Education empowers !

An individual to earn their livelihood and also increases one's awareness on a range of issues. From healthcare, to appropriate social behavior, to understanding one's rights – Junnon Foundation seeks to educate, empower and cultivate better citizens.
